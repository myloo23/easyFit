# Market and Monetization

## Initial audience

- Serious Apple Watch runners.
- Marathon trainees.
- Endurance athletes.
- Data-driven fitness users.

## Competitive landscape

The product may learn from categories served by Apple Health, Apple Fitness, Strava, Garmin Connect, TrainingPeaks, WHOOP, Athlytic, Bevel, Livity, and Runalyze.

Do not clone competitors, copy proprietary formulas, or copy protected interface designs.

## Potential differentiation

- Deep Apple Health and Apple Watch focus.
- Transparent methodology instead of unexplained scores.
- Personal baselines and trend interpretation.
- Strong privacy posture.
- Marathon-specific preparation and durability insight.
- Clear separation between measured data, estimates, predictions, and recommendations.

## Future monetization options

- Freemium personal analytics.
- Subscription for advanced analytics, long-term history, and marathon planning.
- Export-friendly paid product for athletes who value data ownership.

No monetization implementation is planned during the foundation or HealthKit discovery phases.
